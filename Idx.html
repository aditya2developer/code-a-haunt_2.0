Swachhta & LiFE Dashboard: AI-Powered Cleanliness Monitoring Maintaining urban cleanliness is a crucial step towards a healthier and more sustainable environment. The Swachhta & LiFE Dashboard is an AI-driven surveillance platform designed to detect unclean conditions (गंदगी) in real-time, including dead animals, garbage accumulation, sewage overflow, and blockages. Using advanced image processing technology, the system identifies sanitation issues and automatically notifies the concerned municipal authorities for swift action. Accessible from the Divisional Office, this dashboard enables proactive intervention, ensuring that every post office and its surroundings remain clean, hygienic, and aligned with Swachh Bharat and LiFE (Lifestyle for Environment) goals. Together, we can foster a cleaner and healthier future for all.

24*7 Environment Servillance

Surveillance cameras play a crucial role in monitoring public sanitation by detecting garbage accumulation, sewage overflow, and the presence of dead animals. These cameras, often equipped with AI-based image recognition, help authorities identify and address hygiene issues in real-time. By continuously scanning streets, drains, and public spaces, they enable prompt waste disposal, prevent environmental contamination, and reduce health hazards. Additionally, early detection of sewage overflow prevents waterlogging and minimizes the spread of diseases. Integrating such smart monitoring systems improves urban cleanliness and ensures a healthier living environment for communities.
